Asambe

Asambe is a transportation platform designed to connect
users in remote areas or places with scarce transport options, providing them with information on available transportation services through realtime mapping.


It can be difficult to predict when transport will be available. This uncertainty can cause inconvenience and make it difficult to plan other activities.


To solve this problem, Asambe allows drivers to set an active status and share their live location on a map where passengers can view to see where the transport is and estimate when it will reach their location.


Overall, the Asambe app provides a convenient and reliable way for passengers to plan their travel and for drivers to optimize their operations. By leveraging the power of technology, the app can help make transport more accessible and efficient in areas where it's often a challenge

Features

  • As a driver, you can set your active status and share your live location on the map, so passengers can see your availability and location. This includes being able to toggle your active status on/off and having your location automatically updated on the map as you move.

  • As a commuter, you can view the live location of available drivers on a map, so you can estimate when a driver will reach ypur location and plan your travel accordingly. This includes seeing driver markers on the map with their real-time positions and relevant information.

  • Commuters indicate their desire to travel to or from a location by setting their status to "I want to go," so drivers can see the number of commuters interested in traveling and their locations. This helps drivers decide whether it's worth operating and reduces waiting times for commuters.

About

I was deeply moved by the overlooked challenges faced by rural communities and felt compelled to make a significant impact by leveraging my skills to bridge the gap between technology advancements and people living in remote areas. Through personal experiences in my home area, I witnessed the profound impact of limited access to public transportation. The scarcity of transport options meant that once a vehicle left a location, it would only return when it was full. Consequently, if there was no public transport available at the destination, individuals had to wait for the vehicle to fill up and return, causing delays and disruptions to their travel plans.

These struggles became a catalyst for my realization that a solution was needed. I firmly believed that real-time mapping technology and a dedicated platform could revolutionize the way people in rural areas navigate and access essential services. By connecting individuals with available transportation options in their vicinity, this platform, which later became Asambe, aimed to alleviate the challenges they faced. It became my mission to empower rural communities by providing them with a reliable and efficient means of transportation, ensuring they could access vital services and opportunities without the limitations imposed by scarce public transport.
