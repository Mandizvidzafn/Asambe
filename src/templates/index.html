What is Asambe?

Asambe is a transportation platform designed to connect users in remote areas or places with scarce transport options, providing them with information on available transportation services through realtime mapping.

Transport Uncertainty

It can be difficult to predict when transport will be available. This uncertainty makes it hard to plan daily activities.

Driver Location Sharing

Drivers can set their status as active and share live locations, visible on a community-wide transport map.

Smarter Planning

Asambe allows passengers to track availability and routes, helping them plan travel more efficiently.

Why Asambe Was Built

Scarcity of Transport

Rural areas had inconsistent and unpredictable transport, making travel unreliable.

→
Broadcast Demand

Enable commuters to share their intent to travel to help drivers decide when to operate.

→
Lack of Visibility

Passengers had no real-time updates on driver whereabouts or availability.

→
Live Mapping

Implemented a live map for drivers to share real-time locations with users.

→
Trust Barriers

Users needed a secure and reliable way to access and verify services.

→
Twilio Verification

Secured user identity with phone-based authentication for safer interactions.
